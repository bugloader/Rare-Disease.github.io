Amnesia

What is Amnesia?

Amnesia is refering to losing memory. It can be rather information, experience or both. In real life, people with amnesia(also called amnestic syndrome) usually knows their identity, but they have trouble learning new things.

How does it causes?

Amnesia is mostly caused by brain damage, which causes functional lossing for memoring.

How to treat it?

Currently, there isn't an efficient way to treat with it; however, there are way of psychological help and other technologies that can help improving their memory.

Symptoms

After damage on head, people with anterograde amnesia most likely have problem on learning new things.(short time memory), and people with retrograde amnesia most likely have problem remembering previous things.(long time memory)

What happens if I get rabies?

Post-exposure prophylaxis (PEP) is the immediate treatment for bite victims after exposure, and it prevents virus entry into the central nervous system. It consists of extensive washing and local treatment of the wound ASAP after exposure, a potent and effective rabies vaccine that meets WHO standards, and the administration of rabies immunoglobulin (RIG) if indicated. There are 3 categories of contact and recommended PEP procedures. The first one requires no PEP measures if the animal licks on intact skin or if you are feeding or touching animals. The second one, however, requires immediate vaccination and local treatment of the wounds if there is nibbling of uncovered skin, minor scratches, or abrasions. The third one requires immediate vaccination and administration of RIG if the exposure is severe enough.

Do I need to see a doctor?

If you have experience difficulty that you have trouble on memory, and you recently got a head injury or lost conscious, you should see the doctor immidiately.
